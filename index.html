GIVING BACK

Our Social Impact Fund helps hard working people achieve financial independence.

THE BRIDGE FUND

For those with a clear path to success but need a boost to get there, our Bridge Fund offers the support and resources to help them achieve their goals.

Provided eye exams and 90 pairs of lenses and glasses so the women of 5 artisan communities could do their detailed work.

THE HEALTHCARE FREEDOM FUND

Health should never be a barrier to living life to the fullest. Our Healthcare Freedom Fund is dedicated to helping individuals overcome specific health challenges, ensuring they can enjoy their daily lives without hindrances.

Provided 300 customized 3D printed splints for low-income patients with severe burns so they can heal properly.

The Financial Success Program

Making large financial commitments can be scary. Our Financial Success Program empowers individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to buy a new house, save for retirement, or even launch their own business.
